Historical Significance

This kingdom represented the first attempt to unite the South Slavic peoples under a single state. It emerged from the collapse of Austria-Hungary and aimed to create a unified nation of Serbs, Croats, and Slovenes sharing common cultural and linguistic heritage.

Capital & Monarchy

Capital: Belgrade

First King: Peter I (1918-1921)

Second King: Alexander I (1921-1934)
